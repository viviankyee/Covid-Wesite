Common Symptoms

  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness

Less Common Symptoms

  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivitis
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell
  • a rash on skin, or discoloration of fingers or toes

Serious Symptoms

  • difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • chest pain or pressure
  • loss of speech or movement
